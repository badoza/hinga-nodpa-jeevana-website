Hinga Nodpa Jeevana

Directed by Hrishikesh Sangalikar

Marking a significant milestone as Belagavi's first independent feature film, this cinematic piece is a passionate tribute to regional storytelling. Shot entirely in a stark Black and White aesthetic, the film offers a classic cinematic experience that captures the raw essence, dialect, and Javari music of Uttar Karnataka.
