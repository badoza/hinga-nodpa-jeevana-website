The Film

A Landmark Film

Hinga Nodpa Jeevana marks a significant milestone as Belagavi's first independent feature film. Produced under the banner of Agnilochana, this movie is a passionate tribute to regional storytelling.

The entire film is shot in a stark Black and White aesthetic, offering a premium, classic cinematic experience not seen in Kannada cinema for many years.

Culture & Language


  • Dialect: The film is deeply rooted in the culture and tradition of Uttar Karnataka, incorporating the authentic local dialect throughout the narrative.
  • Music: Features fun, vibrant songs composed in the pure Javari language (Belagavi's local tongue), adding a unique flavor and rhythm to the soundtrack.
  • Cast: Showcases the talent of versatile actors who bring authenticity and depth to the Belagavi setting.

*“Our limitations while shooting in black and white turned into art.”* - Hrishikesh Sangalikar (Times of India)
