Agnilochana is an independent production house born from Belagavi — a city rich with cultural heritage yet long underrepresented in regional cinema. We exist to give authentic voice to the stories, dialects, and traditions of Uttar Karnataka.

Every project is driven not by commerce, but by craft. By a deep conviction that regional stories deserve to be told with cinematic honesty and cultural pride.

Founder & Director

Hrishikesh
Sanglikar

A filmmaker rooted in Belagavi, Hrishikesh Sanglikar brings a distinctive cinematic vision shaped by the cultural landscape of Uttar Karnataka. His debut feature, Hinga Nodpa Jeevana, marks a historic milestone as Belagavi's first independently produced feature film — shot entirely in Black & White as both an artistic and cultural statement.

His approach to filmmaking is defined by authenticity: using the local Kannada dialect, incorporating folk music, and working with a team that understands the emotional depth of the region's stories. His vision is not to imitate mainstream cinema, but to carve a new cinematic language — one rooted in the soil of Uttar Karnataka.

"Our limitations while shooting in black and white turned into art."
— Hrishikesh Sanglikar, Director  ·  Times of India
